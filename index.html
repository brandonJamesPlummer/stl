HELLO MUSIC LOVERS ♫

As summer approaches, Saint Louis Missouri is gearing up for some exciting upcoming concerts. The city is known for its vibrant music scene, and music enthusiasts can look forward to a diverse range of artists, genres and venues over the next few months.

Kicking off the concert season is the iconic American rock band, Weezer, on July 17th at the Hollywood Casino Amphitheater. The band, known for hits such as "Buddy Holly" and "Island in the Sun", has been entertaining fans for over 25 years. This will be an unforgettable night for fans of rock music in Saint Louis.

Another artist making their way to Saint Louis is the multi-talented singer, songwriter and actress, Halsey. Performing live at the Enterprise Center on July 30th, Halsey has become one of the most popular contemporary pop stars, with her deeply personal lyrics and unique voice.

On August 11th, Country Music superstar, Chris Stapleton, will take the stage at Hollywood Casino Amphitheater. With a voice that can move mountains and a songwriting style that is both simple and profound, Stapleton has been winning over audiences since he first hit the scene over a decade ago.

The infamous Black Crowes will be playing their first tour in six years and making a stop in St. Louis at the Hollywood Casino Amphitheater on August 23rd. With hits like “Jealous Again” and “Hard to Handle”, this rock band is not to be missed. Fans can expect a high-energy performance, complete with plenty of guitar solos and no shortage of crowd sing-a-longs.

In September, Ozzy Osbourne will take the stage at the Hollywood Casino Amphitheater on the 26th. After cancelling his 2019 tour due to health issues, Ozzy is back and ready to rock. Fans can expect a mix of old favorites as well as newer material from the rock legend's latest album.

Finally, wrapping up the concert season in Saint Louis is pop sensation Justin Bieber, who will be performing at the Enterprise Center on October 17th. Bieber's catchy music and infectious energy have made him one of the most successful pop stars of his generation. This is sure to be a high-energy show, with plenty of opportunities for fans to dance and sing along to his catchy hits.

In conclusion, music lovers in Saint Louis have a lot to look forward to this summer and fall season. With performances from iconic bands and contemporary stars alike, there's something for everyone to enjoy. From rock and country to pop and everything in between, the city will be alive with the sound of music. So make sure to book your tickets fast, because they're sure to sell out soon!
